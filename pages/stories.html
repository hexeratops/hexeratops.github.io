Stories


On the side, I sometimes do a little creative writing. The stories listed here all take place in my world of Cottonwool Pines. These are light-hearted tales about the animal creatures who live there. It started as an imaginative exercise, but I decided these lighthearted tales might be something the world could use.

Not all stories will necessarily have a point or purpose, though many will. The genre may sometimes switch, sometimes being a romance, sometimes just a cozy slice-of-life story about one of the creatures, sometimes a mystery. There may also be times when the story is in the future, or in the past. It's written as a series of chronicles from varying perspectives and time periods.

I genuinely hope you enjoy my stories. I'll be updating off and on as time permits.
